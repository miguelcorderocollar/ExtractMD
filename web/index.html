One-Click Extraction

A floating button appears on supported pages. Click it, and content is copied to your clipboard.

Press Ctrl+Shift+E (Cmd+Shift+E on Mac) to extract content instantly.

Auto-Download

When content exceeds your token limit, it automatically downloads as a .md file.
